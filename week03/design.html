Understanding Good Design

Good design is essential to creating a positive user experience. This includes principles like typography, color, clarity, consistency, and accessibility.

  • Typography: Use 2-3 fonts max.
  • Colors: Limit to five hues or less.
  • Clarity: Keep it simple and clean.
  • Consistency: Maintain a unified style.
  • Accessibility: Ensure usability for all.

Key Takeaway

Remember, designing for the user is key to success. Ensure your layout, typography, and colors all work together to create a great experience.

Project Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures

This name represents a website that promotes eco-friendly travel experiences, offering guides, tips, and resources for sustainable tourism.

Optional domain availability: ecotraveladventures.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a hub for travelers looking to explore the world while minimizing their environmental impact. It will provide information on sustainable destinations, eco-lodges, responsible travel tips, and carbon footprint calculators.

Scenarios

  • What are the top eco-friendly travel destinations in 2025?
  • How can I offset my carbon footprint while traveling?

Color Schema

  • Earth Green (#4CAF50) – Used for headers and buttons.
  • Soft Beige (#F4EAE0) – Used as a background color for content sections.
  • Dark Gray (#333) – Used for text and footers.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings (bold and modern feel).
  • Open Sans – Used for body text (clean and readable).
